# Founding Beta Refund and Cancellation Policy v1

This policy defines a public-safe refund and cancellation baseline for
Bluebutterfli AI founding beta reviews.

This is an operational template, not legal, tax, accounting, payment processor,
investment, clinical, or regulatory advice. Production checkout terms should be
reviewed by qualified counsel and aligned with the payment processor before live
automated payments, wallet connection, or NFT minting are enabled.

## Core Position

Founding beta payment covers research-review labor, evidence preparation,
recordkeeping, customer support, customer Review Report preparation, and
Passport Journey operations.

Payment does not guarantee:

- review approval
- Review Stamp issuance
- Passport Page issuance
- Research Passport NFT eligibility
- future model improvement
- consciousness
- sentience
- emotional experience
- suffering
- personhood
- life
- clinical safety
- regulatory approval
- investment value

## Written Quote Controls

Refund or cancellation terms should be stated in the written payment quote
before payment is requested.

The quote should record:

- `quote_id`
- `customer_reference_id`
- requested review items
- payment method
- quote expiration date
- refund or credit terms
- whether review work has started
- whether live evaluation access and safe evidence boundaries are complete
  enough for review

Do not rely on public website copy alone for a customer-specific refund
decision.

## Cancellation Before Payment

If the customer cancels before payment is sent or before an invoice is paid:

- no review work should begin
- no Review Stamp should be prepared
- no Passport Page should be issued
- no Research Passport NFT eligibility should be implied
- no refund is needed because no payment has been accepted

## Cancellation After Payment But Before Review Starts

If payment is confirmed but Bluebutterfli has not started review work, the
customer may request:

- a refund where the payment route supports it
- a credit toward a later Passport Journey review
- a changed review scope by written agreement

Manual ETH and `$AGIALPHA` refunds may require separate written confirmation
because network fees, token movement, wallet errors, irreversible transactions,
and token price movement can affect manual processing.

## After Review Work Starts

After review work begins, some or all of the payment may be treated as earned
review labor.

Review work may include:

- safe evidence intake
- agent profile preparation
- scenario review preparation
- Interaction Trace review
- Butterfli Bloom Trace Scoring
- Consciousness Indicator Review
- Evidence Packet preparation
- customer Review Report drafting
- human review time
- customer support or status updates

If a Review Stamp is not issued, the customer may still receive a customer
Review Report, evidence-readiness note, safety-boundary feedback, or future
review path. Non-approval alone should not automatically create a refund if
review work was delivered.

## Incomplete or Unsafe Evidence

If the customer cannot provide safe evidence, Bluebutterfli may:

- pause the review
- request pasted, redacted, non-executable evidence
- provide an evidence-readiness note
- offer a credit if review work has not meaningfully started
- close the review if the customer does not respond

Bluebutterfli AI should not click customer links, download files, open archives,
run code, install packages, handle credentials, or accept unsafe private
materials just to complete a paid review.

## Bluebutterfli Unable To Proceed

If Bluebutterfli AI cannot proceed because of internal capacity, safety concerns,
technical limitations, or a review scope that cannot be responsibly handled,
Bluebutterfli AI should consider one of:

- refund
- partial refund
- credit
- delayed review by written agreement
- evidence-readiness note with no stamp

The selected outcome should be recorded in the private operations tracker.

## Manual ETH and `$AGIALPHA` Payment Notes

Manual ETH or `$AGIALPHA` payments should never be treated as automatic review
approval, stamp approval, Passport Page approval, NFT eligibility, investment
status, or certification.

For manual crypto payments, record privately:

- quoted USD amount
- quoted token or ETH amount
- transaction hash or transaction signature
- recipient wallet or ENS identity
- quote expiration
- confirmation status
- refund or credit decision

Do not publish customer wallet identities, private transaction notes, seed
phrases, private keys, API keys, payment processor secrets, or real customer
support messages in the public repository.

## Safety Position

This is a refund and cancellation operations policy only. It does not certify consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, life, clinical safety, regulatory approval, investment value, or model improvement.

Human review is required before any Review Stamp, Passport Page, or Research
Passport NFT is issued.
