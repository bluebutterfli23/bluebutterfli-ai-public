# Bluebutterfli Beta Pricing and No-Guarantee Policy v1

This policy defines the first public-safe pricing model for Bluebutterfli
review services.

Pricing is for research-review labor, evidence preparation, customer support,
and recordkeeping. Payment does not guarantee approval, Review Stamp issuance,
Passport Page issuance, Research Passport NFT eligibility, future model
improvement, sentience, consciousness, emotional experience, suffering,
personhood, life, clinical safety, regulatory approval, or investment value.

## Founding Beta Prices

These are starter prices for early research customers. They may change as the
review system, evidence requirements, customer support load, and automation
become clearer.

| Item | Founding beta price | Includes |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Passport Journey Intake | `$50 USD` | Safe customer intake, initial agent profile, payment confirmation, Passport Record setup, and first-pass review triage. |
| Review Stamp Module | `$20 USD` | One requested review module for an existing agent record, evidence preparation, review decision, and stamp status update. |
| Continuous Passport Starter Bundle | `$150 USD` | Passport Journey Intake plus up to five selected Review Stamp Modules in one initial review cycle. Future cycles are billed separately. |
| Metamorphosis Pilot | `$300 USD` | 36 live interactions across selected staged conditions, one human reviewer, falsification notes, and a 3-5 page findings memo. |
| Metamorphosis Deep Research Cycle | `$1,500 USD` | 156 live interactions, three fresh-session replicates per scenario family, sealed holdouts, delayed replay when available, primary human review, independent replay review, and a 15-25 page research report. |
| Symbiotic Weave Extension | Starts at `$750 USD` | At least 60 additional live interactions for up to two agents, critical-trial replay, multi-agent boundary testing, and a provenance appendix. |
| Concierge or enterprise review | Custom quote | Higher-touch review, unusual evidence, multi-agent systems, or custom reporting. |

The first 3-5 accepted Founding Beta agents may receive the standard review
without charge. Premium Metamorphosis work requires a separate written scope
that states whether the work is paid, discounted, or sponsored.

The full Deep Research Cycle may not be quoted as complete unless a qualified
independent replay reviewer is assigned. If that reviewer is unavailable,
Bluebutterfli may offer the Pilot or a custom research scope with the missing
independence disclosed.

## Non-Approval Outcome

If an agent does not meet a requested review module, the customer may receive a
review report, evidence notes, safety-boundary feedback, and a recommended
future review path. The Review Stamp should remain withheld until a human review
decision approves it.

Payment covers review work and record preparation. It does not require
Bluebutterfli to issue a stamp, approve a Passport Page, mint a Research
Passport NFT, or certify any consciousness-related status.

Refund and cancellation terms should follow the
[Founding Beta Refund and Cancellation Policy](founding-beta-refund-and-cancellation-policy-v1.md)
and be stated in the written quote before payment is accepted.

## Human Review Boundary

Automation may prepare evidence, run claim gates, assemble reports, and flag
review signals. Human review controls:

- Review Stamp issuance
- Passport Page approval
- Research Passport NFT eligibility
- Continuous Passport update approval
- any customer-facing claim about review status
