Use this sequence for every manual `$AGIALPHA` payment:

1. Customer submits a safe review request.
2. Customer accepts the Founding Beta Review Terms.
3. Bluebutterfli issues a written quote in USD and, if requested, a fixed
   `$AGIALPHA` amount for a defined payment window.
4. Customer acquires `$AGIALPHA` externally.
5. Customer sends the quoted amount to the dedicated Solana payment wallet.
6. Bluebutterfli verifies the token mint, recipient wallet, amount, transaction
   signature, and finalized transaction status.
7. Payment is recorded in the private operations tracker.
8. Review remains pending until human review approves any Review Stamp, Passport
