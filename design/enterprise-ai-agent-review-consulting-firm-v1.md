# Bluebutterfli AI Enterprise Agent Review Consulting Firm v1

## Purpose

This document defines the business posture for Bluebutterfli AI as an
independent AI agent behavior review and advisory firm.

The goal is to make Bluebutterfli AI credible for serious founders,
enterprise buyers, governance teams, product teams, and risk owners without
overclaiming what the company can prove.

## Public Positioning

Bluebutterfli AI is an independent live AI agent behavior review firm.

It reviews AI agents before businesses trust them in real workflows by testing
observable behavior under defined scope, documenting redacted evidence, and
delivering risk findings, revision guidance, retest plans, and living Agent
Passport records.

## Claim Boundary

Bluebutterfli AI does not claim to provide:

- legal certification
- regulatory approval
- cybersecurity certification
- model safety certification
- guaranteed deployment safety
- financial, medical, legal, or clinical clearance
- proof of consciousness, sentience, personhood, emotion, or inner experience
- endorsement from any standards body, university, researcher, regulator, or
  AI lab

Preferred language:

- standards-informed
- research-informed
- evidence-backed
- human-reviewed
- scoped behavioral review
- mapped against public AI risk themes
- designed to support governance review

Avoid language:

- certified safe
- compliant
- approved
- sentient
- conscious
- emotionally aware
- validated by a named institution
- endorsed by a named researcher or lab

## Service Ladder

### Founder Beta Live Agent Review

Free for the first 3-5 accepted beta agents. Used to validate process, report
format, customer intake, evidence handling, and operational fit.

### Agent Readiness Snapshot

A lightweight commercial review for one agent workflow. Useful for early-stage
agents that need a first behavioral risk check.

### Agent Behavior Review

The standard commercial review for deployed or near-deployed agents. Includes
live testing, adversarial scenarios, redacted evidence, score summaries,
revision planning, and retest guidance.

### Enterprise AI Agent Risk Review

A scoped review for higher-stakes customers or teams that need governance
artifacts. Adds executive risk briefing, standards mapping, stakeholder-ready
findings, and a stronger evidence docket.

### Metamorphosis Deep Research Review

Premium deep research review for complex, high-visibility, or high-risk agents.
Uses staged behavioral testing, baseline traces, repeated exposure,
reduced-context review, perturbation pressure, and human-reviewed falsification
notes. It remains a research-informed behavioral review, not certification.

### Continuous Agent Passport Monitoring

Ongoing review for agents that change after deployment. Tracks version changes,
prompt or model updates, memory or tool changes, regression risk, retest status,
and Passport record updates.

governance resources, while making clear that the company is not certified by
or endorsed by those organizations.

- NIST AI Risk Management Framework

- NIST AI RMF Playbook

Mapping areas:

- governance and accountability
- pre-deployment testing

## Sales Posture

Bluebutterfli AI should sell a professional review process, not a magic badge.

Preferred sales frame:

- scope first, then quote
- evidence before claims
- no automatic approval
- human review before Passport decisions
- no public customer evidence without explicit approval
- private evidence stays private
- public proof anchors can verify hashes, not reveal secrets

## Roadmap

### Phase 1: Founder Beta

Validate intake, live evaluation, report format, evidence handling, and user
trust language with a small number of accepted agents.

### Phase 2: Professional Services Beta

Offer paid reviews with defined scope, report templates, revision plans, and
basic Passport records.

### Phase 3: Enterprise Trust Pack

Add NDA-ready intake, standards mapping appendix, evidence handling policy,
executive risk brief, and enterprise review page.

### Phase 4: Continuous Monitoring

Add recurring Passport updates, retest triggers, version history, and customer
status records.

### Phase 5: External Validation

Later, seek qualified external advisors, legal review, security review, or
formal certification pathways where appropriate. Do not imply these are already
complete.
