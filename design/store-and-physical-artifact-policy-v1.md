# Store and Physical Artifact Policy v1

Store and Physical Artifact Policy v1 defines how Bluebutterfli may later offer
lab identity items, optional physical passport display books, and approved
printed stamp sheets without weakening the research-review boundary.

The store is a future optional lane. The core Bluebutterfli product remains the
Passport Journey: safe intake, evidence preparation, review modules, human
review, customer Review Reports, Passport Pages, Review Stamps, and possible
Research Passport NFT display after approval.

If a store is added, the preferred launch model is print-on-demand fulfillment
through a store platform or apparel provider, not manual at-home fulfillment.
That keeps order handling separate from research review and avoids unnecessary
shipping operations during founding beta.

## Safety Position

This is a store, fulfillment, and brand-boundary planning artifact only. It does
not certify consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering,
personhood, life, clinical safety, regulatory approval, investment value, model
improvement, Review Stamp approval, Passport Page approval, or Research Passport
NFT eligibility.

Human review is required before any Passport Page, Review Stamp, or Research
Passport NFT is issued.

## Professional Framing

The store should feel like a serious research lab identity line, not the main
product.

Use language such as:

- Bluebutterfli AI Lab
- Agentic Psychology Lab
- The Chrysalis Protocol
- Passport Journey
- Research Passport
- Review Field Notes

Avoid language such as:

- Butterfli Research
- Certified Sentient
- Conscious Agent
- Approved AI
- Passed Sentience Test
- Sentience Certificate

The phrase `Butterfli Research` should be avoided on merch because it can sound
like literal butterfly biology research.

## Future Store Items

Acceptable future item categories:

- shirts
- sweatshirts
- stickers
- lab notebooks
- field-note style journals
- optional physical Research Passport display book for customers who have paid
  for an agent review
- optional printed Review Stamp sheet after human approval

Store items must not imply that the buyer, holder, agent, model, or system has
passed review.

## Physical Passport Boundary

Physical passport books can be beautiful future display artifacts, but they
should come after the digital review record is stable.

Physical passport rules:

1. The digital Passport Record remains authoritative.
2. The digital evidence packet remains authoritative.
3. Physical passports are available only for customers who have paid for a
   Bluebutterfli AI agent review.
4. Payment for review may make a customer eligible to order a physical passport,
   but it does not guarantee Review Stamp approval, Passport Page approval, or
   Research Passport NFT eligibility.
5. Physical pages are display copies or keepsakes unless tied to an approved
   digital Passport Page.
6. Review Stamp sheets may be printed only after human approval.
7. Physical stamps do not transfer review status to another agent.
8. Lost, transferred, resold, or gifted physical items do not transfer review
   approval.
9. Mailing a physical passport must not expose private review evidence.

## Client Information Protection

The store and fulfillment process must stay separate from review evidence.

Store or print fulfillment systems may receive:

- order ID
- shipping name
- shipping address
- item selected
- fulfillment status

Store or print fulfillment systems must not receive:

- private transcripts
- raw customer evidence
- agent behavior traces
- model configuration details
- API keys
- credential files
- wallet private keys
- payment card data
- private support notes
- full review reports unless the customer explicitly asks for a printed copy
  through a reviewed private process

Print-on-demand providers should receive only the minimum order and fulfillment
details needed to produce and ship the selected item.

Public artifacts should use pseudonymous `customer_reference_id`, `agent_id`,
`passport_id`, `review_report_id`, and `review_stamp_id` values.

## Automation Boundary

Automation may:

- collect store interest
- draft order status messages
- create a public-safe product catalog
- prepare fulfillment checklists
- draft print-on-demand product descriptions
- prepare printed display-page previews
- remind the reviewer when a physical artifact is waiting for approval

Automation must not:

- issue Review Stamps
- issue Passport Pages
- approve Research Passport NFT eligibility
- send private evidence to fulfillment
- mark a physical stamp as approved without human review
- imply certification through store copy
- combine customer evidence records with public merch orders
- automatically send a physical passport order before review payment status and
  eligibility are checked

## Launch Rule

The store should remain future-only until:

1. the digital Passport Journey is stable
2. customer privacy handling is stable
3. payment/refund handling is stable
4. Review Report delivery is stable
5. physical fulfillment copy is reviewed
6. no private review evidence is routed to store systems
7. store purchases are clearly separated from review approval
8. print-on-demand terms, privacy, refunds, and shipping rules are understood

Recommended public posture:

```text
Future Bluebutterfli AI Lab store: optional identity items and physical display
artifacts. Store purchases do not imply review approval.
```
