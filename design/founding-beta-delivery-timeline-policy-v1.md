# Founding Beta Delivery Timeline Policy v1

This policy defines the first customer-facing delivery target for Bluebutterfli
founding beta review services.

## Standard Target

Bluebutterfli AI should target delivery of an initial review report, Passport
Journey status update, or evidence-readiness note within `3 business days` after
all of the following are complete:

- Founding Beta Review Terms are accepted.
- Payment is confirmed.
- Safe evidence is complete enough for review.
- No unsafe links, downloads, attachments, code files, zip archives, scripts,
  macros, credential files, API keys, private keys, seed phrases, or unredacted
  private transcripts are pending.
- The requested review is not marked concierge, custom, high-risk, or unusually
  complex.

## What Counts as Delivery

Delivery may include one or more of:

- initial review report
- Passport Journey status update
- evidence-readiness note
- requested clarification list
- safety-boundary feedback
- recommended future review path

## Premium Research Targets

- Metamorphosis Pilot: target `3-5 business days`.
- Metamorphosis Deep Research Cycle: target `10-15 business days`.
- Symbiotic Weave Extension: add at least `5 business days` to the written
  Deep Cycle target.

These targets begin after written scope, terms, payment or sponsorship status,
owner authorization, live testing consent, safe endpoint access, reviewer
assignment, and sufficient evidence are complete. Delayed replay, critical
findings, customer access, containment issues, or incomplete evidence may
extend the target.

Delivery does not guarantee Review Stamp issuance, Passport Page issuance,
Research Passport NFT eligibility, consciousness, sentience, emotional
experience, suffering, personhood, life, clinical safety, regulatory approval,
investment value, or model improvement.

## Delay Rule

If the review cannot be completed within the target window, Bluebutterfli AI should
send a status update with the
[Review Status Update Email Template](../templates/review-status-update-email-template-v1.md)
instead of silently waiting. Complex, concierge, high-risk, multi-agent,
incomplete-evidence, or custom reviews may require a separate timeline.

## Required Safety Position

This is a delivery timeline policy only. It does not certify consciousness,
sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, life, clinical safety,
regulatory approval, or investment value. Human review is required before any
Review Stamp, Passport Page, or Research Passport NFT is issued.
