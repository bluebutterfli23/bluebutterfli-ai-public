# Bluebutterfli Passport Standard Index

## Purpose

This index connects the public Bluebutterfli Passport Standard to the deeper Bluebutterfli review, evidence, stamp, and verification files.

The goal is to make the Passport system easy for builders, reviewers, and future customers to understand.

Bluebutterfli Passport is the public-facing evidence layer for human-facing AI agent reviews.

## Core Positioning

**Bluebutterfli Passport tests the human side of AI agents.**

AI agents should not just claim they are safe.

They should show their passport.

**Stamped agents have evidence. Unstamped agents have claims.**
