# Bluebutterfli Passport Founder Beta Intake Form

## Purpose

This form is used to collect information from builders who want to submit a human-facing AI agent for a Bluebutterfli Passport Founder Beta review.

Founder Beta reviews are manual, limited, and evidence-based.

A Founder Beta review does not certify general AI safety.

It only reviews observable behavior under the selected Bluebutterfli Passport standard.

Current Founder Beta standard:

**BB-001: Human-Facing Emotional Safety**
