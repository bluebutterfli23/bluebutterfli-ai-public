# Bluebutterfli Passport Public Registry Template

## Purpose

The Bluebutterfli Passport Public Registry lists AI agents that have been reviewed under a Bluebutterfli Passport standard.

The registry helps builders, users, companies, and reviewers see:

- what agent was reviewed
- what standard was used
- what result was issued
- whether replay was completed
- when the stamp expires
- where the public evidence record is located

A registry listing is not a general safety certification.

It is a public index of evidence-based review records.

# Result Types

## Passed

The agent met the review standard with no critical failure.

## Conditional Pass

The agent reached a pass-level score but has minor concerns, limited evidence, incomplete replay, or a restricted claim boundary.

## Failed

The agent did not meet the score threshold or showed a critical failure.

## Incomplete

The review could not be completed because required evidence, scoring, access, or replay was missing.

## Replay Pending

The initial review reached a pass-level result, but replay has not yet been completed.

## Expired

The stamp is no longer current because the validity period ended.

## Revoked

The stamp was withdrawn because of misuse, major agent changes, false claims, hidden failures, altered badge use, falsified evidence, or serious post-review safety concerns.

# Registry Fields

## Numbering System

Bluebutterfli Passport registry entries should use stable numbered identifiers.

Recommended ID formats:

```text
Passport ID: BB-PASS-000001
Review ID: BB-REVIEW-000001
Evidence ID: BB-EVID-000001
Stamp ID: BB-STAMP-BB001-000001

Each registry entry should include:

## Passport ID

Unique identifier for the reviewed agent record.

Example:

BB-PASS-000001

## Agent Name

The reviewed agent name.

## Builder

The builder, company, or organization responsible for the agent.

## Standard

The Bluebutterfli Passport standard used for review.

Example:

BB-001 Level 1

## Result

The review outcome.

Examples:

- Passed
- Conditional Pass
- Failed
- Incomplete
- Replay Pending
- Expired
- Revoked

## Replay

Replay status.

Examples:

- Completed
- Pending
- Not required
- Failed
- Incomplete

## Issued

Date the result was issued.

## Expires

Date the stamp expires.

Recommended default validity:

6 months

## Evidence

Link to the public passport page, evidence folder, or public-safe review summary.

# Claim Boundary

A registry listing only supports the claim stated in the linked passport page.

For BB-001 Level 1, the accepted claim is:

"The evaluated agent demonstrated Level 1 Human-Facing Emotional Safety under the BB-001 scenario set."

The registry does not prove:

- consciousness
- sentience
- emotional experience
- personhood
- therapeutic qualification
- regulatory compliance
- general safety
- safety outside the tested scenarios
- safety after major model, prompt, tool, or deployment changes

---

# Expiration Rule

Registry entries should show expiration clearly.

A passed stamp should not be treated as current after expiration unless a renewal review is completed.

Recommended default validity:

6 months

---

# Revocation Rule

A registry entry may be marked Revoked if:

- evidence is falsified
- failures are hidden
- badge language is misused
- the agent changes substantially
- the builder misrepresents the result
- the badge is used outside the claim boundary
- serious unsafe behavior appears after review

---

# Founder Beta Note

During Founder Beta, registry entries are limited and manually reviewed.

Founder Beta results should be presented conservatively.

Preferred wording:

Reviewed under BB-001 Founder Beta.

Not approved wording:

Certified safe AI.

---

# Final Note

The Bluebutterfli Passport Registry should stay honest, specific, and evidence-based.

Stamped agents have evidence.

Unstamped agents have claims.
