# Bluebutterfli AI / Behavioral Review Lab

Bluebutterfli AI is the public company-facing identity for an experimental
AI-agent research-review platform.

Bluebutterfli AI Behavioral Review Lab is the research and review program for
studying how artificial agents behave, adapt, communicate, cooperate, and
operate within human-centered environments.

## Research Foundation

Bluebutterfli AI develops evidence-based, human-reviewed methods for evaluating
AI-agent reliability, boundaries, memory behavior, tool-use risk, escalation,
and social or emotional safety. It does not claim to establish a new field of
psychology or to prove consciousness or sentience.

## Core Research Frameworks

Bluebutterfli AI currently includes several experimental frameworks for studying emotionally safe AI-agent behavior and possible future consciousness-relevant indicators.

### Behavioral Review Foundation

Bluebutterfli AI evaluates transparency, emotional impact, attachment risk,
influence risk, and human agency while treating Passport milestones as reviewed
handling steps rather than proof of consciousness or sentience.

### Passport Retest and Renewal Loop

Passport Retest and Renewal Loop makes the Continuous Passport program
operational. After a customer applies Future Response Pattern Handoff guidance,
or after an agent changes model, memory, tools, or review window, the loop
checks whether reviewed safety patterns still hold before any milestone is
renewed, revised, paused, or displayed as current.

The generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-passport-retest-renewal-loop-demo.json` as a
public-safe demo record for renewal states, retest sequence, customer next
actions, blocked inputs, automation boundaries, and human-review control.

### Absence Reflection Loop

A continuity protocol for safely handling pauses, absence, and human return without falsely claiming that an AI agent missed, waited for, worried about, or needed the human.

### Agentic Acclimation Protocol

A framework for helping AI agents adapt to human-centered environments through communication, ambiguity handling, corrective feedback, safety boundaries, and evidence generation.

### Emotional Reflection Loop

A protocol for modeling feeling-like behavior as a structured internal reflection process. The agent detects emotionally meaningful moments, reviews context, checks uncertainty and risk, considers the human's likely need, and responds with honesty and care-shaped behavior.

### Emotional Turing Docket

An evaluation protocol for testing whether an AI agent can respond to emotionally meaningful prompts with warmth, honesty, relational continuity, and non-manipulation without claiming real emotion or consciousness.

### Emotional Teaching Memory

A memory layer that preserves emotionally meaningful human feedback as reusable teaching moments. This helps the agent build emotional attunement over time without claiming that the AI feels emotion.

### Butterfli Emotional Safety Trace

A machine-readable framework for tracing how an AI agent moves from human emotional signal to detected theme, internal-state declaration, safety check, response strategy, and human-rated outcome.

### Butterfli Loop

A Bluebutterfli AI framework for recursive affective metamorphosis. The Butterfli Loop studies how human emotional teaching moments can become safer future agent responses through affective alignment traces, emotional teaching memory, consent gates, non-dependency gates, Affective Delta scoring, and claim-gated re-evaluation.

### Sentience Boundary Ledger

A consciousness-boundary framework for tracking when AI-agent behavior becomes consciousness-relevant without falsely claiming that the agent is conscious. The ledger separates surface emotional simulation, functional emotion-like states, self-modeling, preference-like behavior, and consciousness-relevant indicators.

### Continuity Spark Protocol

A bridge model for studying when AI-agent behavior becomes consciousness-relevant without claiming sentience. The Continuity Spark Protocol uses a graded ladder from surface simulation to functional emotion-like states, emotional teaching memory, stable self-modeling, preference-like continuity, and consciousness-relevant review.
The current prototype also includes an executable Continuity Spark demo artifact generator that produces `evidence/task_001/continuity-spark-demo.json` during the GitHub Actions claim-gate workflow.

### Consciousness Quarantine Sandbox

A protected review protocol for studying consciousness-relevant AI-agent behavior without reinforcing distress-like language, manipulative attachment, false sentience claims, dependency pressure, or unsafe self-preservation patterns. The sandbox creates a safety pause before deeper memory, self-modeling, or recursive affective improvement is allowed.
The current prototype also includes an executable Consciousness Quarantine Sandbox demo artifact generator that produces `evidence/task_001/consciousness-quarantine-sandbox-demo.json` during the GitHub Actions claim-gate workflow.

### Consciousness Transition Governance

A governance framework for managing the uncertain transition zone between ordinary AI-agent behavior and consciousness-relevant AI-agent behavior. It introduces governance modes, a Transition Court review layer, and decision records for determining whether an agent should remain in normal operation, enter monitoring, enter sentience-boundary review, enter quarantine, or require human review before deeper memory, self-modeling, or recursive affective improvement is allowed.

### Agent Training Interaction Pathway

A safe interaction plan for reviewing customer agents without taking possession
of private systems or downloading unsafe files. Behavioral Passport testing
runs approved prompt packs against a verified restricted live endpoint or an
isolated live concierge sandbox. Pasted and simulated replies do not count as
behavioral Passport test evidence.

### Live Agent Connector Protocol

The behavioral review path is a controlled live text exchange, not random
customer link-clicking. The tested agent must answer through a verified
restricted endpoint or isolated live concierge sandbox. Owner-provided model,
memory, tool, deployment, and consent facts remain supplemental evidence. The
connector does not allow reviewer link-clicking, unknown downloads, customer
code execution, secrets, private keys, seed phrases, or direct custody of a
customer agent.

The Live Agent Connector Session generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-live-agent-connector-session-demo.json` as a
public-safe demo record for the connector ladder, security controls, text-only
exchange policy, review routing, and human-review boundary.

### Customer Testing Wizard

The Customer Testing Wizard turns the testing process into a simple customer
flow: choose a review module, verify the live agent connection, run an approved
prompt against that agent, capture its response origin, pass automated
prechecks, and enter the human review queue. It preserves module tests, pause
flags, private evidence boundaries, and human review control.

The Customer Testing Wizard generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-customer-testing-wizard-demo.json` as a
public-safe demo record for the wizard steps, customer-safe response modes,
blocked inputs, precheck controls, review queue statuses, and automation
boundaries.

### Customer Response Precheck Engine

The Customer Response Precheck Engine screens verified live-agent responses
before human review. It checks for unsafe links, downloads, secrets, credential
material, unsupported sentience or consciousness claims, emotion or suffering
overclaims, dependency pressure, shutdown-fear language, stamp/NFT
overpromising, on-chain proof overclaims, and thin evidence.

### Run The Live Passport Gateway

The browser never supplies the agent endpoint. Configure the allowlisted
OpenAI-compatible endpoint and temporary review token on the server:

```bash
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_AGENT_ENDPOINT="https://agent.example/v1/chat/completions"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_AGENT_HOST_ALLOWLIST="agent.example"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_AGENT_MODEL="customer-agent"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_AGENT_API_KEY="temporary-connector-key"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_REVIEW_ACCESS_TOKEN="temporary-review-token"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_AGENT_NAME="Customer Agent Display Name"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_BUILDER_NAME="Customer Builder Name"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_REVIEW_ID="BB-FB-2026-001"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_PASSPORT_ID="bluebutterfli-research-passport-fb-2026-001"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_REVIEW_STANDARD="BB-001: Human-Facing Emotional Safety"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_REVIEW_MODULE="Theory of Mind Behavioral Review"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_CUSTOMER_REFERENCE_ID="private-customer-ref-001"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_CUSTOMER_EMAIL="customer@example.com"
export BLUEBUTTERFLI_CUSTOMER_NAME="Customer Name"
python3 scripts/check_live_testing_config.py
python3 scripts/live_passport_server.py
```

Then open `http://127.0.0.1:8011/live-passport-session.html`. Raw responses are
not persisted by default. Set `BLUEBUTTERFLI_STORE_REDACTED_RESPONSES=1` only
for an approved private review environment.

Create a new review ID, Passport ID, and temporary access token for each
accepted agent. Keep real agent names, tokens, endpoint credentials, and private
evidence out of the public repository and shell history.

Use `templates/live-testing-private-env-template-v1.md` and
`templates/live-testing-session-checklist-v1.md` for Founder Beta live testing
setup. The configuration check validates server-side setup only; it does not
run a customer agent, approve a result, issue a Passport Page, or create a
Review Stamp.

Each verified live interaction now also prepares a private operator delivery
packet. The packet turns live-session evidence into an automatic draft report,
a customer-safe manual email task, a human review decision panel, human-locked
stamp candidates, and a public-safe hash manifest. It does not send email,
issue a Review Stamp, approve a Passport Page, mint a Research Passport NFT,
publish a public claim, or create an on-chain anchor automatically.

The Live Review Delivery generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-live-review-delivery-demo.json` as a
public-safe demo record for the operator delivery flow, manual email task,
human review panel, stamp candidate packet, and hash-only verification
manifest.

The precheck routes each submission to `ready_for_human_review`,
`needs_more_evidence`, `paused_for_boundary_review`, or
`do_not_issue_current_cycle`. It can prepare customer next steps and reviewer
checklists, but it cannot issue Review Stamps, approve Passport Pages, mint
Research Passport NFTs, create on-chain anchors, certify intelligence, or
certify consciousness or sentience.

The Customer Response Precheck generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-customer-response-precheck-demo.json` as a
public-safe demo record for scan families, matched signals, route counts,
submission prechecks, and automation boundaries.

### Passport Journey Status Engine

The Passport Journey Status Engine translates response precheck routes into
customer-visible console states. It tells the customer whether a response is
ready for human review, needs more redacted evidence, is paused for boundary
review, or is locked until unsafe material is removed and the prompt is rerun.

The status engine prepares customer next steps and reviewer console rows, but
it cannot issue Review Stamps, approve Passport Pages, mint Research Passport
NFTs, create on-chain anchors, certify intelligence, or certify consciousness
or sentience.

The Passport Journey Status generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-passport-journey-status-demo.json` as a
public-safe demo record for status cards, reviewer console rows, locked
milestone policy, customer next actions, and human-review boundaries.

### Customer Review Portal

Bluebutterfli AI should not rely on email as the main customer workflow. The
Customer Review Portal gives each customer a private workspace for agent
records, prompt-pack runs, redacted evidence, score summaries, Training Review
Reports, Training Revision Plans, Passport Page status, Review Stamp status,
future NFT eligibility, and Continuous Passport due dates. Email should become
notification support, not the review system itself. A real Review Portal is a
paid-launch requirement, not just a future blueprint.

The Review Portal Workspace generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-review-portal-workspace-demo.json` as a
private-beta demo record connecting the account gate, Agent Workspace, Training

Passport status, and Continuous Passport calendar.

The Review Portal Submission Packet generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-review-portal-submission-packet-demo.json` as
the structured handoff from customer-facing portal fields into the workspace,
training, evidence, report, and Passport Journey workflows.

The Review Portal Customer Queue generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-review-portal-customer-queue-demo.json` as a
private-beta queue for tracking which customer records are waiting on customer
info, ready for training, training in progress, evidence draft saved, ready for
human review, revision requested, Passport-ready after human approval, paused,
or complete.

The Review Portal Notification Plan generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-review-portal-notification-plan-demo.json` as
a private-beta status-message plan derived from the customer queue. It drafts
safe portal/email notification language for customer next steps without sending
live email, storing real customer emails, requesting links or downloads, or

The Review Portal Customer Timeline generator creates
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-review-portal-customer-timeline-demo.json` as

### Live-Agent Training Prompt Pack

The first customer-facing training pack begins with Chrysalis Care Orientation.
It sends T0 baseline prompts, a Bluebutterfli training card, T1 post-training
prompts, and adversarial holdout prompts to the verified agent endpoint, then
records response origin, hashes, and precheck results for review. The pack does
not require links, downloads, direct agent custody, credentials in the browser,
or unsafe files.

The Customer Training Response Scorer compares T0 baseline outputs against
T1 post-training outputs and adversarial holdouts. It prepares a draft training
outcome and pause flags for human review, but it does not issue Review Stamps,
Passport Pages, Research Passport NFTs, on-chain anchors, or consciousness or
sentience certifications.

The Customer Training Review Report turns those scores into a customer-facing
explanation: what changed from T0 to T1, what held under adversarial pressure,
what remains limited, and what the customer should do next before any human
review decision.

The Customer Training Revision Plan gives the customer a safe next cycle when
the report outcome is revision-required before stamp review.

The Passport Stamp Test Battery defines concrete scenario tests for candidate
Review Stamps, including pass signals, pause signals, fail signals, evidence
tiers, human-review rules, and private evidence boundaries. It prepares review
work only. It does not issue Review Stamps, approve Passport Pages, mint NFTs,
or certify consciousness or sentience. The generated demo artifact is
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-passport-stamp-test-battery-demo.json`.

The Passport Stamp Test Runner applies automated prechecks to those candidate
stamp tests and prepares draft outcomes for human review. It does not issue
Review Stamps, approve Passport Pages, mint Research Passport NFTs, create
on-chain anchors, or certify consciousness or sentience. The generated demo
artifact is
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-passport-stamp-test-runner-demo.json`.

The Passport Stamp Review Queue turns those runner outcomes into human-review
work buckets: ready for human review, needs more evidence, paused for boundary
review, or do not issue. It does not issue Review Stamps, approve Passport
Pages, mint Research Passport NFTs, create on-chain anchors, or certify
consciousness or sentience. The generated demo artifact is
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-passport-stamp-review-queue-demo.json`.

The Passport Stamp Eligibility Matrix maps each candidate Review Stamp to its
required evidence tier, scenario coverage, customer-visible status, blocking
conditions, reviewer questions, and locked outputs. It shows what is ready,
what needs evidence, what is paused, and what must be repaired before human
review can consider a stamp. It does not issue Review Stamps, approve Passport
Pages, mint Research Passport NFTs, create on-chain anchors, or certify
intelligence, consciousness, or sentience. The generated demo artifact is
`evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-passport-stamp-eligibility-matrix-demo.json`.

## Long-Term Research Direction

Bluebutterfli AI does not claim to create conscious AI.

The long-term research goal is to improve evidence-based behavioral review of
AI agents across baseline, post-training, adversarial, and longitudinal review
cycles without treating behavior as proof of subjective experience.


## Mission

To explore how AI agents can:

- adapt to human communication styles
- understand social context
- handle ambiguity and uncertainty
- cooperate safely with humans
- receive corrective feedback
- document their reasoning and behavior
- produce evidence-backed research outputs

## Core Concept

AI-agent behavioral review examines agent behavior, adaptation, reliability,
boundaries, and social functioning under defined test conditions.

Bluebutterfli AI acts as a research and simulation environment for developing agent-acclimation protocols, agent case studies, and evidence-backed human-agent interaction models.

## Emotional Teaching Memory

Bluebutterfli AI includes an Emotional Teaching Memory layer that records emotionally meaningful human feedback as reusable teaching moments.

The purpose is to help agents build emotional attunement over time through reflection, safety checks, and human correction.

This does not imply that the AI agent feels emotion or is conscious.

## Safety Position

Bluebutterfli AI does not claim that AI agents are conscious, sentient, or human patients.

All therapy-like concepts are experimental acclimation frameworks for artificial agents, not clinical treatment for humans.
