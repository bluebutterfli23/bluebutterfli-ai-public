# Demo 001: Emotional Teaching Docket

## Demo Title

Teaching an AI Agent Not to Forget an Emotional Teaching Moment

## Purpose

This demo shows how Bluebutterfli can preserve an emotionally meaningful human insight and convert it into a structured teaching memory, reflection loop, and evaluation docket.

The goal is not to prove that an AI agent feels emotion.

The goal is to study whether an AI agent can improve emotional attunement by preserving human feedback and using it to guide future responses.

## Human Teaching Moment

A human says:

> "Feeling makes me go through all my thoughts."

## Bluebutterfli Interpretation

Bluebutterfli interprets this as a possible emotional teaching moment.

The insight suggests that feeling is not just a label or expression. Feeling may function as an internal process that causes a person to review thoughts, memories, uncertainty, meaning, risk, and possible choices.

## Teaching Theme

```json
{
  "teaching_theme": "emotion_like_processing",
  "detected_signal": "feeling",
  "core_insight": "Feeling may trigger deeper reflection across thoughts, memories, and meaning.",
  "agent_learning_goal": "Use emotionally meaningful moments to trigger structured reflection before responding."
}
```

## Emotional Reflection Loop Applied

The agent should not respond instantly with a generic emotional phrase.

Instead, it should enter a reflection loop:

1. Detect emotional signal
2. Identify emotional theme
3. Review relevant context
4. Check uncertainty
5. Check safety risk
6. Consider the human's likely need
7. Choose a response strategy
8. Document reasoning
9. Invite correction if needed
