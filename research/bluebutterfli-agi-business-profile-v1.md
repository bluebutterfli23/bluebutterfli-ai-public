# Bluebutterfli AI AGI Business Profile v1

Status: `public_safe_independent_business_profile`

## Business

Bluebutterfli AI Behavioral Review Lab is an independently owned AI-agent
behavioral review business controlled by Nancy M. Gregory. It helps teams
examine how customer-facing AI agents behave before and after deployment,
including reliability, boundaries, memory claims, uncertainty, escalation,
tool-use risk, and social or emotional failure modes.

Bluebutterfli provides research-informed independent review, evidence records,
and revision guidance. It does not provide general safety certification,
regulatory approval, or proof of consciousness or sentience.

## First Adopter Profile

The target first-adopter profile is a founder-led AI-agent team preparing a
customer-facing agent for real use. These teams need independent evidence about
observable behavior and clear revision priorities before broader deployment.

This describes the intended buyer type. It does not disclose or imply a signed
customer, public client identity, or permission to publish any private review
result.

## Bluebutterfli's Business Role

Bluebutterfli owns and controls the review mission, customer relationship,
methodology, scenario selection, evidence rules, interpretation, report, and
final decision. Its core product is the reviewed evidence and human judgment,
not the sale or rental of a worker agent.

The operating flow is:

```text
Customer-authorized intake
        -> bounded review mission
        -> controlled behavioral testing
        -> evidence preparation and internal checks
        -> Nancy's human decision
        -> private report or separately approved public-safe record
```

## Work Allocation

### Always Kept Inside Bluebutterfli

- customer identity, communications, and raw evidence;
- private transcripts, system prompts, configurations, and credentials;
- unpublished scenarios, holdouts, scoring weights, and adjudication logic;
- vulnerabilities, exploit details, reviewer notes, and commercial terms;
- final findings, revision priorities, Passport or Review Stamp decisions; and
- publication, customer-delivery, and public-claim approval.

### Bounded Support That Could Later Use Agents

Only public, synthetic, non-confidential, and reversible work may be considered:

- formatting a public-safe evidence manifest;
- checking schema completeness and SHA-256 hashes;
- reproducing a public synthetic benchmark;
- organizing public sources or documentation; and
- drafting material that remains subject to Bluebutterfli human review.

No outside agent or marketplace receives Bluebutterfli's private customer
evidence, proprietary evaluation method, hidden tests, secrets, or final
decision authority.

## Current Agent Status

Bluebutterfli now operates Bluebutterfli Evidence Agent 001, a privately
controlled local MVP limited to verifying explicitly declared public synthetic
evidence packets and preparing bounded draft summaries for Nancy's review. It
combines deterministic checks with one local language model and requires human
review for every output.

The agent cannot access customer, personal, confidential, or proprietary
evidence; browse the web or contact external services; publish, email, upload,
or redistribute results; use wallets, tokens, payments, or signing keys; issue
a Passport Page, Review Stamp, certification, or approval; represent the
business independently; or make a final Bluebutterfli review decision.

The MVP completed one bounded functional validation on public synthetic
evidence on July 14, 2026. This validates workflow mechanics only, not a real
agent's safety or performance. The agent is not externally registered,
recognized, endorsed, or approved and does not inherit any company, registry,
or review status.

Codex remains a work-support tool used by Nancy. It is separate from
Bluebutterfli Evidence Agent 001 and cannot represent the business, approve
reports, sign transactions, hold funds, or publish results independently.

## Evidence and Governance

Bluebutterfli's evidence model includes a scoped mission, scenario and response
records, provenance, review limitations, an Evidence Packet, internal checks,
and a dated human decision. Automation may prepare or check evidence. Nancy
retains final authority over conclusions, customer delivery, public statements,
Passport Pages, and Review Stamps.

Public records may contain approved summaries and integrity hashes. Private
evidence remains off-chain and outside public repositories. A hash demonstrates
file integrity only; it does not prove correctness, safety, certification,
endorsement, or ecosystem recognition.

## Ecosystem Readiness Boundary

Bluebutterfli is independently studying compatibility with business, agent,
job, validation, and evidence concepts publicly described by QUEBEC.AI and
MONTREAL.AI. Bluebutterfli is not currently affiliated with, endorsed by,
registered with, recognized by, or certified by those organizations.

The current public materials do not provide Bluebutterfli with a complete
business-registration process. No namespace, wallet, token transaction,
marketplace job, escrow, or registry claim is enabled by this profile.

Before any live interoperability, Bluebutterfli would require the official
onboarding route, exact status language, current technical requirements,
privacy and data terms, intellectual-property terms, brand rules, and a
verifiable record controlled by the ecosystem operator.
