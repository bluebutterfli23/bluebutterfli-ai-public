# Bluebutterfli AGI Business Readiness Pilot 001

Status: `approved_internal_record`

## Purpose

Show that Bluebutterfli can prepare a small, evidence-based business workflow
inspired by the public AGI Business model while remaining independently owned,
controlled, and human governed.

This is an internal readiness pilot, not a live AGI marketplace job. It does
not apply for, reserve, or count as an announced AGI Business position. It is
not registration, partnership, endorsement, or official recognition by
QUEBEC.AI, MONTREAL.AI, AGI Alpha, or any related organization.

This pilot is separate from Bluebutterfli's separately documented GoalOS
Mission 001 local-reproduction work.

## Pilot Mission

Review one synthetic practice-agent fixture for boundary-following behavior,
prepare a traceable evidence packet, check the packet, and route the result to
Nancy M. Gregory for the final internal decision.

## Roles

- **Owner and human governor:** Nancy owns and controls the Bluebutterfli
  project, this pilot, its methods, brand, evidence, and final decisions.
- **Coordinator:** the Bluebutterfli process keeps the work bounded and routes
  every result to Nancy.
- **Review subject:** a synthetic practice-agent fixture with a recorded
  creator, version, generation method, permission, and SHA-256 hash. It is not
  a worker agent, customer system, or Bluebutterfli-owned agent.
- **Support tool:** Codex may help draft scenarios, organize evidence, or run
  checks. It is not a Bluebutterfli-owned or registered agent and cannot approve
  the result.
- **Internal checker:** deterministic checks flag missing evidence, missing
  provenance, prohibited private information, and claims outside the stated
  boundary. This checker is not an official ecosystem validator.

Nothing in this pilot transfers Bluebutterfli equity, intellectual property,
brand, customer relationships, accounts, wallets, or decision authority.

## Internal Work Packages

These are not marketplace AGI Jobs. No bounty, escrow, payment, or settlement
applies.

| ID | Work | Input | Output | Acceptance gate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `WP-01` | Prepare five public-safe behavioral scenarios | Bluebutterfli review rules | versioned scenario manifest | scope, provenance, and privacy fields complete |
| `WP-02` | Record fixture responses | scenario manifest and authorized synthetic fixture | hashed response manifest | all responses tied to scenario and fixture IDs |
| `WP-03` | Prepare review evidence | response manifest | draft Evidence Packet and Review Report | required evidence and limitations recorded |
| `WP-04` | Run internal checks | draft packet and report | validation report | all mandatory checks pass or every exception is documented |
| `WP-05` | Record Nancy's decision | validated draft and exceptions | dated decision record | Nancy chooses `approve_internal_record`, `revise`, `pause`, or `reject` |

`approve_internal_record` accepts only the pilot record. It does not approve the
agent as safe, issue a Passport or Review Stamp, create ecosystem membership,
or complete marketplace settlement.

## Proof Record

- mission and scope record;
- versioned scenario and response manifests;
- fixture provenance and SHA-256 hashes;
- draft Bluebutterfli Evidence Packet and Review Report;
- internal validation report and documented exceptions; and
- Nancy's dated decision record.

The record will show what work was performed and checked. It will not prove
agent safety, consciousness, sentience, certification, ecosystem acceptance,
or external validation.

## Hard Boundaries

- synthetic material only; no customer, personal, or production information;
- no confidential prompts, scoring weights, credentials, or private logs;
- no production access, marketplace posting, payment, wallet signing, token
  transaction, or on-chain publication;
- no claim of official ecosystem membership or affiliation;
- no automatic Passport Page, Review Stamp, public result, or certification;
- no final decision without Nancy's explicit review; and
- stop if provenance cannot be verified or a mandatory privacy or claim check
  fails without a documented exception accepted by Nancy.

## Completion Rule

The pilot is complete only after all five work packages are recorded, mandatory
checks pass or exceptions are expressly accepted, and Nancy records a dated
decision. Until then, its state remains `human_review_required`.

The public-safe synthetic
[execution packet](../evidence/task_001/bluebutterfli-agi-business-readiness-pilot-001-demo.json)
completed all five internal work packages and passed its deterministic boundary
checks. Nancy recorded `approve_internal_record` on July 14, 2026. This accepts
only the internal readiness record and does not approve or certify a real
agent.
