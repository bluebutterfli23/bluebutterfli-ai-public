# Bluebutterfli Website Launch Checklist v1

Use this checklist before publishing `bluebutterfliai.com` publicly.

The website should launch as a research-review platform preview, not as a live
checkout, automatic certification system, wallet app, NFT minting site, or
customer evidence portal.

## Domain and Brand

- Primary domain is `bluebutterfliai.com`.
- Supporting or legacy domain `bluebutterfli.com` may redirect to
  `bluebutterfliai.com` or to a research-lab section.
- Public company-facing name is Bluebutterfli AI.
- Header, page title, and footer use Bluebutterfli consistently.
- Public copy says Agentic Psychology Lab or research-review platform.
- No public copy claims to certify consciousness, sentience, emotion,
  suffering, personhood, or life.

## Launch Mode

- Website status is preview or early access.
- Payment processing is disabled unless terms, refund policy, and private
  operations are ready.
- Wallet connection is disabled.
- Research Passport NFT minting is disabled.
- Review Stamp issuance is human-review only.
- Customer accounts are not enabled in public preview mode.
- Paid review launch requires a private Customer Review Portal before customers
  are asked to manage real review work.
- Store checkout and physical passport ordering are disabled unless fulfillment
  and privacy boundaries are ready.
- No one can place an automatic order without human confirmation.

## Privacy and Customer Safety

- No real customer emails, names, transcripts, private evidence, API keys,
  credential files, wallet secrets, payment card data, or private downloads are
  stored in the public repo.
- Intake asks for pasted text, redacted summaries, owner answers, or concierge
  review requests.
- Reviewers are not required to click customer links or download customer files.
- Screenshots remain secure-preview-later only.
- Customer-safe review request template is available before paid review.
- Store or physical passport fulfillment must not receive private review
  evidence, raw agent records, transcripts, behavior traces, API keys,
  credential files, wallet secrets, or support notes.

## Payment and Wallet Copy

- Ordinary card or invoice payment is described as the first practical route.
- Founding beta review terms are accepted before payment is requested.
- Written payment quote is issued before card, invoice, ETH, or `$AGIALPHA`
  payment is expected.
- Founding beta delivery target is stated as an initial review report, Passport
  Journey status update, or evidence-readiness note within `3 business days`
  after terms, payment, live evaluation access, and safe evidence boundaries are
  complete.
- ETH payment to `bluebutterfliai.eth` is optional and manual after terms are
  confirmed.
- `$AGIALPHA` Solana payment is optional and manual after terms are confirmed.
- Bluebutterfli AI does not sell `$AGIALPHA`, provide custody, provide trading
  advice, or provide bridging advice.
- `$AGIALPHA` is strictly a payment method, not access, approval, stamp status,
  or NFT eligibility.
- `$AGIALPHA` payment verification checks token mint, recipient wallet, amount,
  transaction signature, and finalized transaction status.
- `$AGIALPHA` copy is payment routing only, not an investment claim.
- Payment-confirmed next-steps email is available and does not include secrets,
  attachments, downloads, private transcripts, or approval promises.
- Review status update email is available when the 3-business-day target needs
  more time, live evaluation access, safe evidence boundaries, or human clarification.
- Review delivery email is available and distinguishes approved, not-approved,
  pending, and future-review outcomes.
- Email is notification support only once the Customer Review Portal is live;
  it should not be the main paid-review workspace.
- Payment, intake, or NFT ownership does not imply review approval.

## Research Passport and Review Stamps

- Research Passport NFT is described as future or after review approval.
- Passport Record comes before Review Stamps.
- Review Stamps are non-transferable review records tied to a specific agent.
- Passport Pages and Review Stamps require human review.
- NFT ownership alone does not imply review approval.
- Store purchases, shirts, sweatshirts, physical passport books, or printed
  display items do not imply review approval.

## Store and Physical Artifacts

- Store remains future-only unless deliberately enabled.
- Print-on-demand fulfillment is preferred over manual shipping.
- Store copy uses Bluebutterfli AI Lab, Agentic Psychology Lab, The Chrysalis
  Protocol, Passport Journey, or similar AI-agent research language.
- Store copy avoids `Butterfli Research` because it can sound like literal
  butterfly biology research.
- Store items do not imply consciousness, sentience, review approval, stamp
  approval, Passport Page approval, or Research Passport NFT eligibility.
- Physical Research Passport books are display copies only; the digital Passport
  Record and evidence packet remain authoritative.
- Physical Research Passport books are available only for customers who have
  paid for a Bluebutterfli AI agent review.
- Printed Review Stamp sheets are offered only after human approval.
- Store handling follows the Store and Physical Artifact Policy.

## Safety Language

The site must include:

```text
Bluebutterfli AI artifacts are research and safety-review artifacts only. They do
not certify consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering,
personhood, life, clinical safety, regulatory approval, or investment value.
```

## Repository Checks

- GitHub Actions are green.
- Claim-gate tests pass in GitHub Actions.
- Generated evidence artifacts are demo or public-safe only.
- `.gitignore` keeps `outputs/`, `evidence/`, and `memory/` out of ordinary
  source commits.
- Website files do not include real customer data.
- Launch checklist is linked from the dashboard and website blueprint.
- Founding beta launch readiness status is linked from the dashboard and
  website blueprint.

## Public and Private Code Boundary

The public repo may include:

- research docs
- public schemas
- static website preview
- demo agents
- generated demo artifacts
- public-safe templates
- claim-gate tests

Keep private:

- production backend code
- customer tracker or customer database
- real customer evidence, transcripts, emails, and support notes
- payment processor integration code
- wallet connection code
- NFT minting code until deliberately enabled and reviewed
- admin dashboards
- API keys, private keys, access tokens, `.env` files, and deployment secrets
- real automated review orchestration for paying customers
- smart contract deployment scripts and signing keys

The public repo should show the research model and demo flow. The private
system should handle real customers, money, wallets, authentication, and
operations.

## GitHub Security Hardening

- Enable two-factor authentication on the GitHub account.
- Keep repository admin access limited.
- Enable GitHub secret scanning and push protection where available.
- Enable Dependabot alerts or dependency review where available.
- Use branch protection or repository rules before accepting collaborators.
- Require GitHub Actions to pass before merging important changes.
- Do not commit `.env` files, secrets, private keys, payment keys, wallet keys,
  or customer data.
- Use least-privilege tokens for deployments.
- Keep uploads, accounts, payments, wallet connection, and NFT minting disabled
  until they are deliberately built and reviewed.

## Pre-Launch Decision

Do not publish as a live paid service until:

- review pricing is final
- refund and cancellation policy is drafted
- privacy and contact policy is drafted
- manual review workflow is ready
- human reviewer decision checklist is ready
- customer tracker is private
- founding beta customer operations playbook is ready
- Customer Review Portal is ready before accepting paid review work at scale
- payment confirmation process is defined
- NFT minting process is deliberately enabled later
- store and physical artifact policy is drafted

## Safety Position

This checklist is a launch-readiness tool only. It does not certify
consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, or life.
This launch checklist does not certify consciousness.
Human review is required before any Review Stamp, Passport Page, or Research
Passport NFT is issued.
