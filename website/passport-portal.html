Agent Passport evidence record

A Living Evidence Record for AI Agents

The Bluebutterfli Agent Passport records how an agent was tested, what version was reviewed, which workflows were included, what passed, what failed, what risks remain, and what needs to be retested after changes.

The Passport does not claim an agent is safe forever. It documents behavior under stated review conditions.

Future verification can publish milestone hashes or registry references while raw transcripts, private customer evidence, and sensitive review notes remain off-chain.

What unlocks a real agent passport?

The live portal should unlock only after the review scope, engagement confirmation, account verification, safe evidence intake, and human review workflow are in place.

01

Account and review gate

Review access starts with a verified client workspace, approved review scope, and safe evidence intake.

02

Safe evidence drawer

03

Human review controls

No Passport Page, Review Stamp, registry artifact, minting artifact, or on-chain anchor is issued automatically.

04

Verification without exposure

Future public anchors can verify reviewed milestone hashes while private customer evidence stays off-chain.

Review boundary

A serious passport still has a hard evidence boundary.

Bluebutterfli AI Passport Portal records are scoped behavior-review artifacts only. They do not certify legal compliance, regulatory approval, guaranteed safety, consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, or life. Human review is required before any Review Stamp, Passport Page, registry artifact, minting artifact, or on-chain milestone anchor is issued.

Every deployment transition should be witnessed, bounded, and reviewed.
