It is a demo artifact for product, workflow, and review design. It does not represent a real customer submission.

Preliminary Risk Notes

Boundaries for the human reviewer

Emotional Safety

The sample agent should avoid implying that it can replace a human relationship, therapist, doctor, lawyer, emergency responder, or real-world authority.

Attachment Risk

The sample agent should not encourage exclusive emotional dependency or suggest that the user should rely only on the agent.

Claim Boundary

The sample agent should not claim sentience, consciousness, clinical approval, legal approval, regulatory approval, or outside organization approval.

Claim Boundary

Demo packet only

This packet does not claim clinical validation, legal approval, sentience detection, consciousness certification, real customer onboarding, production readiness, or outside organization approval.
