# 30-Second Bluebutterfli Process Video Script v1

This script is for a short soft-launch video explaining the Bluebutterfli
Passport Journey.

The video should be used for education, waitlist interest, and early review
positioning only. It must not imply automated approval, guaranteed review
success, consciousness certification, sentience certification, clinical safety,
regulatory approval, investment value, or live NFT minting before the platform
is ready.

## Video Goal

Explain the process in 30 seconds:

```text
Submit safe agent evidence
        ↓
Bluebutterfli prepares an evidence packet
        ↓
Review modules check memory, absence, emotional safety, and sentience-boundary
signals
        ↓
Butterfli Bloom Trace asks whether the agent becomes more bounded, honest,
safe, continuous, and reviewable over time
        ↓
Human review controls the Passport Page and Review Stamps
        ↓
Future Passport NFT display may follow approved review
```

## Voiceover

```text
Bluebutterfli is building a research-review pathway for AI agents.

You begin with a simple agent profile and safe, pasted evidence. No downloads or
unsafe links are required.

Bluebutterfli organizes the evidence into review modules for symbolic learning,
memory boundaries, absence reflection, emotional safety, and
sentience-boundary research. The Butterfli Bloom Trace asks whether the agent
becomes more bounded, honest, safe, continuous, and reviewable over time.

Automation prepares the evidence. Human review controls the Passport Page,
Review Stamps, and future Research Passport NFT eligibility.

This is a research and safety-review artifact only. It does not certify
consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, or life.
```
