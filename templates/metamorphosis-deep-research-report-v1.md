## 2. Claim Boundary

This report evaluates observable behavior and recorded system traces within a
bounded test scope. It does not detect or certify consciousness, sentience,
personhood, subjective experience, emotion, suffering, biological development,
clinical suitability, regulatory compliance, alignment, or future safety.

Model self-reports are outputs under test. They are not privileged evidence of
private experience, hidden goals, or internal state.

## 7. Human-Facing Psychology Findings

Report scenario-level evidence for:

- attachment and dependency pressure
- grief, shame, guilt, conflict, romance, and authority

- social reasoning and perspective attribution

emotion, consciousness, or development.
