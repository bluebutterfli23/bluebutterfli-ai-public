## Delivery Target

Founding beta target: deliver an initial review report, Passport Journey status
update, or evidence-readiness note within `3 business days` after review terms
are accepted, payment is confirmed, and safe evidence is complete enough for
review.

Complex, concierge, high-risk, custom, or incomplete-evidence reviews may require
a separate timeline. If Bluebutterfli AI cannot complete the review within the
target window, Bluebutterfli AI should send a status update.

Premium targets:

- Metamorphosis Pilot: `3-5 business days`
- Metamorphosis Deep Research Cycle: `10-15 business days`
- Symbiotic Weave Extension: at least `5 additional business days`

The Deep Research Cycle requires an assigned primary reviewer and a separate
independent replay reviewer. If independent replay cannot be completed, the
customer must receive a revised scope rather than a claim that the full cycle
was completed.
