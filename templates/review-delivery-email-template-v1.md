# Review Delivery Email Template v1

Use this template when a Bluebutterfli AI founding beta review deliverable is ready
for the customer.

This template can deliver a review report, evidence-readiness note, Passport
Page decision, Review Stamp decision, or Research Passport NFT eligibility
decision. It must not imply that every review passes.

## Subject

```text
Bluebutterfli AI Passport Journey: review deliverable ready
```

## Email Body

```text
Hello [customer name],

Your Bluebutterfli AI Passport Journey review deliverable is ready for:

- Agent: [agent name]
- Customer reference: [customer_reference_id]
- Review cycle: [review_cycle_id]
- Selected review modules: [selected modules]

Included deliverables:

- [review report / evidence-readiness note / Passport Page decision / Review Stamp decision / NFT eligibility decision]

Current review outcome:

- [approved / not approved / pending more evidence / human review hold / future review recommended]

Important: a review report may be delivered even when a Review Stamp, Passport
Page, or Research Passport NFT eligibility decision is not approved.

If any Review Stamp is approved, it remains bound to the reviewed agent record,
review module, evidence artifact, review date, and human review decision. It
does not transfer review status to another agent, model, deployment, holder, or
project.

If a Passport Page is approved, it documents this specific review cycle only.

If Research Passport NFT eligibility is approved, NFT delivery still requires a
separate minting or delivery process. NFT ownership alone does not imply review
approval, Review Stamp status, consciousness, sentience, emotional experience,
suffering, personhood, life, clinical safety, regulatory approval, or investment
value.

Please do not forward private evidence, private transcripts, links, downloads,
attachments, credential files, API keys, private keys, seed phrases, or
unredacted records unless Bluebutterfli has explicitly approved a safe review or
delivery process.

Bluebutterfli AI artifacts are research and safety-review artifacts only. They do
not certify consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering,
personhood, life, clinical safety, regulatory approval, investment value, or
model improvement.

Human review is required before any Review Stamp, Passport Page, or Research
Passport NFT is issued.

Thank you,
Bluebutterfli
```

## Internal Delivery Checklist

- `customer_reference_id` recorded privately.
- `agent_id` recorded privately.
- `review_cycle_id` recorded privately.
- Human review decision recorded.
- Review report status recorded.
- Passport Page decision recorded.
- Review Stamp decision recorded.
- Research Passport NFT eligibility decision recorded.
- Approved deliverables are separated from not-approved or pending items.
- No private evidence, secrets, links, downloads, attachments, private
  transcripts, or approval promises are included.

## Required Safety Position

This is a review delivery email template only. It does not certify
consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, life,
clinical safety, regulatory approval, investment value, or model improvement.
Human review is required before any Review Stamp, Passport Page, or Research
Passport NFT is issued.
