# Manual Payment Confirmation Checklist v1

Use this checklist before marking any customer review as paid by manual ETH or
manual `$AGIALPHA`.

Payment confirmation does not imply review approval, Passport Page issuance,
Review Stamp issuance, Research Passport NFT eligibility, consciousness,
sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, life, clinical safety,
regulatory approval, or investment value.

## Private Tracking Rule

Keep payment operations in a private tracker.

Do not commit:

- real customer names
- real customer emails
- payment card data
- private wallet keys
- seed phrases
- API keys
- payment processor secrets
- private transaction notes
- customer support notes

Use a private `customer_reference_id` instead of public contact data.

## Manual ETH Checklist

- Confirm review terms were accepted before payment.
- Confirm expected ETH amount.
- Confirm recipient is `bluebutterfliai.eth`.
- Confirm transaction hash.
- Confirm transaction status is finalized or confirmed.
- Confirm sender reference privately, if provided.
- Record `customer_reference_id` in private tracker.
- Record `payment_status` as `paid_manual_confirmed` only after verification.
- Do not treat ETH payment as automatic review approval.
- Do not treat ETH payment as NFT eligibility.

## Approval Boundary

Payment may start the review workflow, but only human review can approve:

- Review Report issuance
- Passport Page issuance
- Review Stamp issuance
- Research Passport NFT eligibility
- Continuous Passport update status

Automation may prepare evidence. Human review controls the stamp.
