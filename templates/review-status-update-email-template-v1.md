# Review Status Update Email Template v1

Use this template when a Bluebutterfli AI review needs more time, more safe
evidence, human review clarification, or a separate timeline.

This template is for communication only. It is not a review approval, Review
Stamp, Passport Page, Research Passport NFT eligibility decision, or
consciousness-related certification.

When the review deliverable is ready, use the
[Review Delivery Email Template](review-delivery-email-template-v1.md).

## Subject

```text
Bluebutterfli AI Passport Journey: review status update
```

## Email Body

```text
Hello [customer name],

This is a Bluebutterfli AI Passport Journey status update for:

- Agent: [agent name]
- Customer reference: [customer_reference_id]
- Review cycle: [review_cycle_id]
- Selected review modules: [selected modules]

Your review is still active.

We are sending this update because the review needs one or more of the following:

- more safe evidence
- human review clarification
- additional safety-boundary review
- additional time due to complexity
- a separate concierge, custom, high-risk, or incomplete-evidence timeline

Current status:

- [current status]

Next expected step:

- [next expected step]

Target follow-up:

- [target follow-up date or timeline]

Please do not send links, downloads, attachments, code files, zip archives,
scripts, macros, credential files, API keys, private keys, seed phrases, or
unredacted private transcripts unless Bluebutterfli has explicitly approved a
safe review process.

This status update does not guarantee review approval, Review Stamp issuance,
Passport Page issuance, Research Passport NFT eligibility, model improvement,
consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, life,
clinical safety, regulatory approval, or investment value.

Human review is required before any Review Stamp, Passport Page, or Research
Passport NFT is issued.

Thank you,
Bluebutterfli
```

## Internal Send Checklist

- `customer_reference_id` recorded privately.
- `agent_id` recorded privately.
- `review_cycle_id` recorded privately.
- Review remains active.
- Reason for delay is recorded.
- Target follow-up date or timeline is recorded.
- No secrets, links, downloads, attachments, private transcripts, or approval
  promises are included.

## Required Safety Position

This is a review status update email template only. It does not certify
consciousness, sentience, emotional experience, suffering, personhood, life,
clinical safety, regulatory approval, or investment value. Human review is
required before any Review Stamp, Passport Page, or Research Passport NFT is
